Please Explore Our Municipality's Cultural heritage

To us, Cultural heritage is the heritage of tangible and intangible heritage assets of a group or society that is inherited from past generations. Not all heritages of past generations are "heritage"; rather, heritage is a product of selection by society.

We, As a society/Municipality are keen to use our Arts and Culture to Generate income, and bridge the Unemployment gab, We will do this by allowing members of this municipality to register and showcase their talents, culture and all that they can offer. And while they do that, We as a society will help them generate income with just that. For example: A person that can do crafted earrings and bags can employ youth and teach it to do the work, then the more Arts and Culture is exposing their small companies the more they grow to get more clients and more clients means more money.
